Home Sweet Home: enjoy the holiday with hot sale

Ideally perched on the golden crescent of An Bang beach – one of the world’s 25 most beautiful beaches voted by TripAdvisor Asia, a mere 30-minute drive from Danang International Airport, Citadines Pearl Hoi An comprises 581 well-designed rooms and apartments, complemented by sophisticated furnishing for an unparalleled weekend getaway, honeymoon or business trip.

Our hotel rooms and apartments range from superior room to three-bedroom premier suite that are well-appointed with a selection of amenities from Wi-Fi, to LCD television and electronic safe. The serviced residence is part of an integrated complex that will help you and your family settle into your new home whether you are staying for a few days or years. Guests will also be spoilt for choice by the sheer range of unique facilities, from 1,600 sqm ocean front swimming pool to tennis court and fitness centre.

Citadines Pearl Hoi An is managed by The Ascott Limited, a member of CapitaLand. It is one of the leading international lodging owner-operators with more than 760 properties in over 190 cities spanning more than 30 countries across Asia Pacific, Europe, the Middle East, Africa and the USA. Its portfolio of serviced residence and hotel brands includes Ascott The Residence, The Crest Collection, Somerset, Quest, Citadines, lyf, Préférence, Vertu, Harris, Citadines Connect, Fox, Yello and POP!. Experience your choice of lifestyle in the comforts of your apartment complemented by flexible services. Because life is about living.
